Les’t Talk Data Science with R !!!

During the first meeting of the Algiers R user group, entitled; «Let’s Talk Data Science With R» organised the 14th August, 2020 Mohamed El Fodil Ihadadden introduced the Algiers R user group to the audience, then he gave a brief introduction of his batata package, as a first presentation.
The second presentation was hosted by Nassim Kaddour, he gave us an introduction to the dash package on R, an alternative to shiny. Then Mohamed El Fodil Ihadadden, came back to make the third and last presentation on corazon another package he developed.
